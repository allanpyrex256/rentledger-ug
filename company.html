About Us

We make rent management easier for everyday landlords.

The platform is designed around the way local landlords already work: room-by-room rent tracking, MoMo and cash payments, caretaker handovers, tenant balances, WhatsApp reminders, and public listings for vacant spaces.

Our Focus

Give landlords one place to see who paid, who is late, which rooms are free, and which listings are live to tenants.

Our Promise

Keep the product clear, affordable, and practical for landlords who may not be technical.

Our Market

Rooms, shops, boys quarters, apartments, and houses across Kampala, Wakiso, Mukono, and beyond.

Privacy Policy

Your landlord, tenant, property, and payment records stay focused on operating your account.

RentLedger UG uses account data to provide the dashboard, support users, secure access, process subscriptions, and help landlords publish public rental listings when their plan allows it.

Data We Handle

Names, phone numbers, property details, unit records, rent payments, expenses, support requests, subscription details, and public listing information.

How We Use It

To run landlord accounts, calculate balances, show reports, send reminders, process payments, and display public listings chosen by landlords.

What We Do Not Do

RentLedger UG does not sell landlord or tenant records. Public listing details are shown only when a landlord publishes them.

Account Security

Users should protect passwords, use correct phone/email details, and report suspicious account activity through support.

Terms

Simple terms for using RentLedger UG.

By creating an account or using a demo, landlords agree to use the platform responsibly, keep account details accurate, and avoid publishing false or misleading rental listings.

Subscriptions

Starter, Professional, and Enterprise plans are billed monthly after the trial. Public listings unlock on Professional and Enterprise plans.

Public Listings

Landlords are responsible for the accuracy of rental prices, photos, location, availability, and contact details shown publicly.

Verified Badge

Verified badges are reviewed and granted by the super admin. A request does not guarantee approval.

Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellation, duplicate charge, failed collection, and refund requests can be reviewed through support with the relevant payment reference.
